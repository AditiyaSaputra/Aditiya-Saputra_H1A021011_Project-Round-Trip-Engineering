Problem Statement

Book Shop Management System is an information system that helps manage data in bookstores. This system can help super admins to fully manage applications, manage books, manage receipts and help systems user. Then from the customer can make payments from the book to be purchased, can search for books, and see the purchase history. Then shopkeepers can manage inventory, create invoices, and manage bills. And from the super admin, system users, customers, and shopkeepers can create accounnt from the bookstore website, and can log in and out of accounts, change profiles, and change passwords.
